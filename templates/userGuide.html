Application User Guide

This application is designed to predict whether a knee in an uploaded image shows signs of osteoarthritis. It also visualizes the key regions of the knee that contribute to the model's predictions.

Home Page

The Learn More About the Model section provides detailed information about the model, including its architecture, performance results, and the visualization techniques used to interpret predictions.

In the Select an Image field, users can upload an image of a knee for analysis. The image must be in PNG, JPG, or JPEG format.

In the Select Visualization Methods field, users can choose one or more visualization methods to highlight the critical regions of the knee used by the model in its analysis. Selecting at least one method is required.

To obtain a prediction:

  • Upload a knee image in the required format.
  • Select at least one visualization method.
  • Click the Upload button.
The prediction time varies based on the chosen visualization methods. For faster results, users primarily interested in the prediction (without detailed visualizations) are recommended to select the Grad-CAM method.

Results Page

On the results page, users can view:

  • The probability of osteoarthritis detection.
  • The prediction indicating whether the knee is diseased.
  • Visualizations of the model's interpretation, based on the selected methods.

Users can download a summary of the analysis, including displayed information and images, by clicking the Download Report button. The report is generated in PDF format.
